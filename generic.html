About Us

Our Services To Restraunts

When the customers come he or she will scan the QR and it will take him or her to the menu from there they can directly order there menu.After the order they can pay the bill from online method available there or pay at counter.This is will help through the low or no maintenance cost for the restraunts.Consumers will give rating and this rating will help the restraunts for advertisement.
